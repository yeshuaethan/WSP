Lotus

Website Redesign - Project Analysis

Site Intent

There’s a lot of music out there, and it’s easy to get into this habit of only attending shows for bands or musicians with which we’re familiar. Where’s the curiosity? The discovery of new sounds? That open spirit often associated with music?

Lotus is a localized live music discovery app designed to help you find live shows, with a specific focus on a user’s defined location.

My goal is to create a modern, responsive design with easy navigation and semantic markup. The new design will allow visitors to easily get directions. The new design will also make it easy to see upcoming events and bands playing locally.

Core Functionality

Allows users to easily have access to all nearby artists/bands show times and locations. Users can also filter search fort the artists they are searching for and purchase tickets at supporting locations.

Be the first to know about concerts

Competitor Comparison:

Bandsintown Concerts provides a personalized concert calendar and a way to track your favorite artist and discover new touring musicians, based on your musical preferences and location, so you never miss another live show.

Timbre is focused on local band discovery, originated from the company’s CTO, Matt Bridges. Last May, Bridges attended a local hackathon focused on connecting and disconnecting communities.

Songkick tracks your favorite bands so you never miss them live. Instantly create a personalized concert calendar based on your bands and your location by scanning your music library, your Google Music account, or your Last.fm app.

Targeted Audience

Music and local band enthusiasts who are interested in seeing local artists/bands. Age groups leans more towards the ages of 16-40. Target audience also includes people who aren’t willing to travel long distances or spend an excess amount of money.
