Club Hours and Directions

Our hours are from 11:30am till closing on Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday. The club is conveniently located near the 528 Exit off I-95 at 15420 SW 136th St #42, Miami, FL 33196.

From I-95 South: Take Florida's Turnpike and Florida 821 Toll S/Homestead Extension of Florida's Turnpike to SW 120th St in The Crossings. Take exit 19 from Florida 821 Toll S/Homestead Extension of Florida's Turnpike

From Florida Turnpike: Take Florida 821 Toll S/Homestead Extension of Florida's Turnpike to SW 120th St in The Crossings. Take exit 19 from Florida 821 Toll S/Homestead Extension of Florida's Turnpike
